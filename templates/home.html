Tackling the Challenge of Sensory Decline

Prevalence of Sensory Decline

By age 65, nearly 40% experience some form of hearing loss. 1 in 3 seniors over 65 suffer from vision impairment.

Impact on Daily Life

Unaddressed sensory impairments can increase fall risk by 50%.

Early Detection Benefits

Early detection of sensory health issues can improve outcomes by up to 70%.
